Faith as foundation, not formality.

The Church is where I find purpose beyond work. I'm committed to bringing traditional values into contemporary contexts—making faith accessible without diluting what matters.

My role

  • Catechist for young people (12-24 years) in my parish since 2015
  • Weekly meetings with a group of about 15 young people
  • Part of the pastoral council for youth ministry
  • Sunday Mass is non-negotiable—it's where the week begins

What I do with them

  • We read scripture and discuss what it means for their lives—not abstract theology
  • We talk about real questions: relationships, doubt, meaning, choices
  • We organize retreats, service projects, and moments of reflection
  • I listen more than I teach. Their questions are often better than my answers.
